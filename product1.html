Premium Orthopedic Support

The Flo Mattress Ergo™ is engineered with advanced memory foam technology that conforms to your body's natural curves while providing targeted support to pressure points. Our multi-layer construction ensures optimal spinal alignment throughout the night.

The top comfort layer features cooling gel-infused memory foam that regulates temperature, while the high-density base foam provides durable support. The breathable cover wicks away moisture for a dry, comfortable sleep surface.

  • Reduces pressure points by up to 30% compared to traditional mattresses
  • Distributes body weight evenly to prevent tossing and turning
  • Ideal for back, side, and stomach sleepers
